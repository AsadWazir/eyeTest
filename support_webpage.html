Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I use the eye training games? +

For best results, we recommend playing the eye training games 2-3 times daily. Each session should last 5-10 minutes. The Eye Focus Challenge can be played during screen breaks, while the Color Memory Match is perfect for mental warm-ups or cool-downs.

Are the eye exercises safe for everyone? +

Yes, our eye exercises are designed to be safe for healthy individuals. However, if you have any existing eye conditions, recent eye surgery, or experience discomfort during exercises, please consult with your eye care professional before continuing.

Can I use VisionPro AI if I wear glasses or contacts? +

Absolutely! VisionPro AI works perfectly with corrective lenses. In fact, wearing your prescribed glasses or contacts during exercises ensures you're training your vision at its optimal clarity level.

How do I track my progress effectively? +

Your progress is automatically tracked in the app. Check your weekly statistics, exercise streaks, and improvement graphs in the Progress section. We recommend reviewing your analytics weekly to see trends and adjust your training routine accordingly.

What should I do if I experience eye strain? +

If you experience eye strain, take an immediate break and use the 20-20-20 rule: look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds. Adjust your screen brightness, ensure proper lighting, and consider shorter exercise sessions. If strain persists, consult an eye care professional.

How do the nutrition recommendations work? +

Our nutrition section provides evidence-based food recommendations for eye health. Tap any food card to learn about its benefits, add items to your meal planner, and track your daily intake of eye-healthy nutrients like omega-3s, lutein, and vitamins A, C, and E.

Troubleshooting Guide

🎮 Games Not Responding

  • 1Close and restart the VisionPro AI app completely
  • 2Check that your device has sufficient available storage (at least 1GB)
  • 3Ensure you're running the latest version of the app
  • 4Restart your device if the issue persists
  • 5Try playing in airplane mode to rule out connectivity issues

📊 Progress Not Saving

  • 1Verify you're signed in to your VisionPro AI account
  • 2Check your internet connection for cloud sync
  • 3Complete exercises fully - don't exit mid-session
  • 4Manually sync by pulling down in the Progress section
  • 5Contact support if data loss continues

⏰ Reminders Not Working

  • 1Check that notifications are enabled in your device settings
  • 2Verify VisionPro AI has notification permissions
  • 3Ensure "Do Not Disturb" mode isn't blocking alerts
  • 4Review your reminder settings within the app
  • 5Try disabling and re-enabling notifications

🔧 App Performance Issues

  • 1Close other apps running in the background
  • 2Lower graphics quality in app settings if available
  • 3Update to the latest iOS/Android version
  • 4Free up device storage space (recommend 2GB+)
  • 5Reinstall the app as a last resort
