Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities

📅 Last Updated: December 15, 2024

👁️ Welcome to VisionPro AI

At VisionPro AI, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our eye health and vision training application.

🛡️ Our Promise: Your health data stays private and secure. We never sell your personal information to third parties, and we use industry-leading security measures to protect your data.

📊 Information We Collect

To provide you with personalized eye health insights and training programs, we collect the following types of information:

  • Eye test results and vision assessment data
  • Exercise performance and progress tracking
  • App usage patterns and preferences
  • Device information (screen size, resolution for accurate testing)
  • Optional profile information (age, gender for personalized recommendations)
  • Nutrition tracking and dietary preferences
  • Screen time and break reminder settings

Note: All health data collection is optional, and you can choose which information to share with the app.

🎯 How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to enhance your VisionPro AI experience:

  • Provide personalized eye health recommendations
  • Track your progress and improvement over time
  • Customize training difficulty and exercise routines
  • Send relevant health tips and reminders
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Generate comprehensive health reports
  • Ensure optimal app performance on your device

🔐 Data Security & Protection

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

🔒

End-to-End Encryption

All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols

🏠

Local Storage

Most data is stored locally on your device, giving you complete control

🔐

Secure Servers

Our servers use advanced security protocols and regular security audits

👤

Anonymous Analytics

Usage analytics are anonymized and cannot be traced back to you

⚖️ Your Privacy Rights

You have full control over your personal information and privacy settings:

  • Access: View all data we have collected about you
  • Modify: Update or correct your personal information
  • Delete: Remove your account and all associated data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Disable data collection features at any time
  • Control: Manage notification and sharing preferences
💡 Easy Controls: All privacy settings are accessible directly within the app under Settings > Privacy & Data.

🤝 Data Sharing Policy

We are committed to keeping your data private and secure. Here's our strict data sharing policy:

🚫 We Never Share:
• Personal health data with advertisers
• Individual user information with third parties
• Identifiable data for marketing purposes
• Your email or contact information without consent

Limited Sharing: We only share anonymized, aggregated data for research purposes to improve eye health understanding, and only with your explicit consent.

🍪 Cookies & Tracking

VisionPro AI uses minimal tracking to provide the best user experience:

  • Essential cookies for app functionality and login sessions
  • Performance cookies to optimize app speed and reliability
  • Analytics cookies to understand user behavior (anonymized)
  • No third-party advertising or tracking cookies

You can manage cookie preferences in your device settings or within the app's privacy controls.

👶 Children's Privacy

VisionPro AI is designed to be family-friendly while protecting children's privacy:

🔒 Special Protections for Children:
• Parental consent required for users under 13
• Limited data collection for minors
• No behavioral advertising to children
• Enhanced security measures for young users

Parents can review and manage their child's data through the family settings section of the app.

🔄 Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes:

  • You'll receive an in-app notification
  • We'll email you about important changes (if you've provided an email)
  • The updated policy will be posted on our website
  • Continued use of the app indicates acceptance of new terms
